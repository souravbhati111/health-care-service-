Our Story

Who We Are

MediCare was founded in 2009 with a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves access to high-quality, compassionate healthcare. What started as a small clinic has grown into a full-service healthcare centre with over 50 specialists across multiple disciplines.

We serve thousands of patients each year, providing everything from routine check-ups to complex diagnostic and rehabilitation services — all under one roof and with one consistent standard of excellence.
