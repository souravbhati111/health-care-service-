FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a referral to book an appointment? +

No, most of our services are available without a referral. You can book directly through our contact form or by calling us.

What insurance plans do you accept? +

We accept most major insurance plans. Contact our team and we'll verify your coverage before your appointment.

How long does a typical consultation take? +

Initial consultations typically take 30–60 minutes depending on the service. Follow-up appointments are usually 20–30 minutes.

Are my health records kept confidential? +

Absolutely. We are fully HIPAA compliant and your records are protected with the highest security standards.

Can I get a same-day appointment? +

Same-day appointments may be available depending on the service and our schedule. Call us directly for urgent needs.
