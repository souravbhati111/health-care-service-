What We Offer

All Services

🩺

General & Family Consultation

Comprehensive health assessments, routine check-ups and personalised care plans for patients of all ages.

  • Annual health assessments
  • Chronic disease monitoring
  • Vaccination & immunisation
  • Referrals to specialists
🔬

Diagnostic & Imaging Services

Advanced diagnostic tools for accurate and timely identification of health conditions.

  • Blood & urine lab tests
  • X-ray & ultrasound imaging
  • MRI & CT scanning
  • ECG & cardiac monitoring
🦾

Rehabilitation & Physical Therapy

Tailored recovery programmes designed to restore movement, strength, and independence.

  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Sports injury recovery
  • Physiotherapy sessions
  • Occupational therapy
🧠

Mental Health & Counselling

Compassionate psychological support services for individuals, couples and families.

  • Individual therapy sessions
  • Anxiety & depression support
  • Stress management
  • Cognitive behavioural therapy
🌿

Preventive Care & Wellness

Proactive health screenings and lifestyle guidance to keep you healthy before problems arise.

  • Cancer screenings
  • Cholesterol & diabetes checks
  • Nutrition counselling
  • Lifestyle health coaching
💊

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term, coordinated care for patients living with ongoing health conditions.

  • Diabetes management
  • Hypertension monitoring
  • Asthma & COPD care
  • Medication management
